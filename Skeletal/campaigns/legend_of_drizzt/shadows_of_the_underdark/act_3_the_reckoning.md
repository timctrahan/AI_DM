# ACT 3: THE RECKONING (Levels 6-8)

---

## STORY GATES

```yaml
GATE_3.1_THE_STORM_BREAKS:
  trigger: "Threat makes its move"
  what_happens: |
    The threat acts - attacks the settlement, makes a power grab,
    or forces the party to act NOW.
  objectives:
    - Defend what matters
    - Execute the plan (or improvise)
    - Survive the opening confrontation
  completion: "The final conflict has begun"

GATE_3.2_THE_CHOICE:
  trigger: "Mid-climax decision point"
  what_happens: |
    A crucial choice that defines who the party has become:
    - Sacrifice something/someone to ensure victory?
    - Show mercy to a defeated enemy?
    - Accept help from a dark source?
    - Reveal themselves to protect others?
  objectives:
    - Face the choice
    - Make a decision
    - Live with the consequences
  completion: "Party has defined their moral identity"

GATE_3.3_RESOLUTION:
  trigger: "After the choice, moving toward finale"
  what_happens: |
    The final confrontation with the threat.
    The nature reflects the party's journey.
  objectives:
    - Defeat or resolve the threat
    - Determine the fate of their community
    - Decide their future
  completion: "Campaign concludes"
```

# CAMPAIGN ENDINGS

```yaml
ENDINGS:
  triumphant: |
    Threat defeated, community saved, party has a home.
    They've proven that drow can choose differently.

  bittersweet: |
    Threat defeated but at great cost.
    The community survives but is changed.
    Party has lost something irreplaceable.

  pyrrhic: |
    Threat defeated but community destroyed.
    Party survives but must start over.

  dark: |
    Threat defeated through dark means.
    Party has become something troubling.
    Victory, but at what cost?

  surface: |
    Party chooses to seek the surface world.
    The Underdark story ends, but a new one begins.
```
