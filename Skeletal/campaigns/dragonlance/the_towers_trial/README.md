### Skeletal Campaign Framework

This campaign uses the **Skeletal DM Kernel** approach:

- **Story Gates** define objectives, not scriptsâ€”the AI generates the journey
- **Archetype Pointers** let AI render authentic characters from training knowledge

- **Modular structure** that's easy to modify or extend

### Why "Skeletal"?

Traditional modules are **complete skeletons with flesh attached**â€”every room described, every NPC voiced, every encounter scripted. You read what's written.

Skeletal campaigns are **bones only**. The AI adds the flesh in real-time:
- Gate says "confront the dragon" â†’ AI generates the confrontation
- Archetype says "grumpy dwarf veteran" â†’ AI renders the authentic character

**Result:** The structure ensures story coherence. The AI ensures every moment feels alive.
